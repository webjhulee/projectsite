Podcast Ep.1: Introduction and Background Research

This episode of the podcast features Carol Lu and Erica Hwang on the introductory background research of how music in involved with video games and the research results of its influence on the player. These experiments form a foundational model for how this experiment was created.

Podcast Ep.2: Research Methodology and Procedures

This episode of the podcast features John Lee on the details, procedures, and a brief discussion on the results on the data. Podcast Ep.3: Future Implications

This episode of the podcast features Peter Zarakas on the future implications of the experiment regarding music and performance. The expansion of music and performance extends beyond video games, as video games are not the only type of objective for observing levels of performance.
